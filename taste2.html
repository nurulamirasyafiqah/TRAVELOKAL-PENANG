Top 7 Dining Hotspots in Penang

Explore the best dining experiences Penang has to offer.


Laksa Kuah Pekat

Indulge in a bowl of thick, flavorful laksa broth served with rice noodles, fresh vegetables, and a hint of tangy lime. This is a must-visit for those craving authentic Penang-style laksa.

Location: Jalan Mahsuri, Bayan Baru, 11900 Bayan Lepas, Pulau Pinang

Contact Number: 014-250 4956

Operating Hours: 10:00 AM - 8:30 PM (Monday-Sunday, Friday Closed)

Nasi Kandar Deens Maju

A beloved nasi kandar spot renowned for its flavorful curries and perfectly spiced fried chicken. Locals and tourists alike queue up daily for their signature dishes that pair beautifully with aromatic steamed rice.

Location: 170, Jalan Gurdwara, 10300 George Town, Pulau Pinang

Contact Number: 012-425 2421

Operating Hours: 12:00 PM - 10 PM (Monday-Sunday, Friday Closed)

Gani Famous Pasembur

This vibrant dish combines fresh vegetables, tofu, seafood fritters, and boiled eggs, drenched in a sweet and spicy peanut sauce.

Location: Medan Renong, 3, Padang Kota Lama, Jalan Tun Syed Sheh Barakbah, 10200 George Town, Penang

Contact Number: 017-202 0460

Operating Hours: 11:00 AM - 1:00 AM (Monday-Sunday)

Penang Road Famous Teochew Chendul

Beat the tropical heat with this iconic dessert, featuring shaved ice, pandan jelly, creamy coconut milk, and sweet gula Melaka (palm sugar). A refreshing treat that’s synonymous with Penang!

Location: 435B, Lebuh Pantai, George Town, 10300 George Town, Pulau Pinang

Contact Number: 04-262 6002

Operating Hours: 9:00 AM - 6:30 PM (Monday-Sunday)

Hameed Pata Special Mee Sotong

This iconic dish features fried noodles cooked with a spicy, tangy squid sambal. Located at Esplanade Food Court, it’s the ultimate comfort food for spice lovers looking for a unique twist.

Location: Esplanade Park, 5, Lebuh Light, Georgetown, 10300 George Town, Penang

Contact Number: 013-431 9384

Operating Hours: 11:00 AM - 8:00 PM (Monday-Saturday, Sunday Closed)

Ida Mee Udang Sungai Dua

Known for its fresh and succulent prawns, Ida Mee Udang serves mouthwatering prawn noodles in a flavorful broth. The combination of sweetness and umami in every bite makes this worth it to try!

Location: 3640, Jln Sungai Dua, 13800 Butterworth, Pulau Pinang

Contact Number: 017-472 2109

Operating Hours: 1:00 PM - 1:00 AM (Monday-Sunday, Teusday Closed)

Nasi 7 Benua Koo Boo Café

A unique dining experience where diners can sample seven different types of rice in one meal, each infused with distinct flavors and colors. Pair this with their delicious side dishes for a truly unforgettable feast.

Location: Jalan Tun Dr Awang, Taman Tunas Muda, 11900 Bayan Lepas, Pulau Pinang

Contact Number: 012-501 6412

Operating Hours: 11:00 AM - 4:00 PM (Monday-Saturday, Sunday Closed)


Additional Dining Hotpots for Western Food Hunters!


Pak Mat Western Cafe

For those looking for a fusion of Western and Malaysian flavors, Pak Mat Western serves hearty meals like chicken chop, lamb steak, and spaghetti with a local twist. Affordable and delicious, it’s a hidden gem you’ll want to discover.

Location: No. 6 Jalan Industri Tangkas 2 Taman Industri Tangkas Kampung Sekolah, Juru, 14000 Bukit Mertajam, Pulau Pinang

Contact Number: 011-3615 2643

Operating Hours: Open 24 hours (Monday-Sunday)
